# Research and split panes

Research notes and attachments remain in the research root and are searchable
without becoming manuscript content. Use the split workspace to pin a research
note while navigating or editing another document. Pane focus controls which
editor receives navigation, formatting, and search commands; the panes are
symmetric.

Attachments are copied into the project under UUID-derived safe filenames.
Display names are metadata only. Missing, escaped, symlinked, oversized, or
active-content assets are rejected or shown with an actionable warning.
