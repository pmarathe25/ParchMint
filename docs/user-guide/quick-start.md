# Quick start

> Read when creating and navigating a first project.

1. Launch ParchMint and choose **New project** or **Open project**.
2. For a new project, name it and choose its parent folder.
3. In the binder, right-click **Manuscript** to add a section or document.
4. Select a document to open it in the focused editor. Right-click items to
   rename them or open **Properties**; summaries can also be edited from Cards.
5. Drag binder items to reorder or nest them. Use the disclosure controls to
   expand and collapse sections.
6. Split an editor right or down from its header. Drag a binder item onto an
   editor edge to split in that direction, or onto its center to open it there.
7. Open **Cards** to expand, reorder, or reparent the manuscript hierarchy.
8. Search from the top bar; use `Ctrl/Cmd+F` inside the current document.
9. Use the project-actions button to save, export, replace, or change settings.

Structural undo is separate from editor text undo. Deleting a binder item moves
it to project trash instead of immediately erasing its canonical files.

For prepared content, open [`examples/tour`](../../examples/tour).
